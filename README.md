This interactive dashboard was developed to analyze and visualize the factors influencing bike-sharing demand. It summarizes the key findings from a comprehensive data exploration, focusing on temporal patterns and environmental factors.

**Key Features:**
* **Hourly Rental Trends:** Comparison of peak usage times between working days and non-working days.
* **Seasonal & Weather Impact:** Visualization of how different weather conditions and seasons affect rider behavior.
* **Period Analysis:** Aggregated view of rentals across different times of the day (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night).
* **Correlation Matrix:** An interactive heatmap exploring the relationships between numerical variables like temperature, humidity, and windspeed.
